Rules & Strategies

Welcome to the Class of 2024/2025! Here are some rules and strategies to help you maintain a positive and engaging learning environment.

  • Be respectful and considerate of others' time and interests.
  • Encourage open communication and collaboration among students.
  • Stay organized and prioritize tasks.
  • Take breaks and enjoy the process of learning.
Encourage Questions and Discussion

As a class leader, it's essential to encourage questions and discussion among students. This can be done through various ways, such as:

  • Encouraging students to share their thoughts and ideas.
  • Offering constructive feedback and encouraging students to improve.
  • Inviting students to share their perspectives and opinions.
  • Using discussion boards and online forums to facilitate open and honest dialogue.
